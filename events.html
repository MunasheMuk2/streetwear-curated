Top upcoming street fashion and trainers events you can’t miss

Whether you’re hunting for the latest kicks or wanting to immerse yourself in the urban fashion scene, these events bring together fans, collectors, and fashion-forward individuals. Here’s a look at the top trainer and street fashion events in London you should mark in your calendar.

1. KicksnCoffee Festival: A relative newcomer to Glasgow’s sneaker scene, KicksnCoffee has quickly made a name for itself by combining sneaker culture with a laid-back social vibe and is one of the most important streetwear events in Glasgow.

When: Annually.
Location: Glasgow - City in Scotland

2. Manchester Fashion Week – street style edition : Manchester Fashion Week isn’t just about high-end designer shows – the street style on display during the event often steals the spotlight.

When: Twice a year (February and September).
Various venues across Manchester.

3. Sneakers unboxed at the design museum: For those looking to dive deep into the evolution of sneaker culture, Sneakers Unboxed is an essential stop

Annually, with changing exhibitions.
Location: Design Museum, Kensington.
