Upcoming streetwear events you can’t miss

Whether you’re hunting for the latest kicks or wanting to immerse yourself in the urban fashion scene, these events bring together fans, collectors, and fashion-forward individuals.

Your Guide to the Hottest Streetwear Events

1. KicksnCoffee Festival: Popular in Glasgow’s sneaker scene, KicksnCoffee has quickly made a name for itself by combining sneaker culture with a laid-back social vibe and is one of the most important streetwear events in Glasgow.

When: Annually.
Location: Glasgow - City in Scotland

2. New Event: Manchester Fashion Week – street style edition : Manchester Fashion Week won't just be a high-end designer shows – the street style on display during the event is promising to steal the spotlight.

When: Twice a year (February and September).
Location: Various venues across Manchester.

3. Sneakers unboxed at the design museum: For those looking to dive deep into the evolution of sneaker culture, Sneakers Unboxed is an essential stop

When: Annually, with changing exhibitions.
Location: Design Museum, Kensington.
